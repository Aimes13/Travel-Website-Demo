Attractions

Victoria Falls

A natural wonder, this is the main attraction, a stunning cascade also known as "The Smoke that Thunders" for its roaring sound and mist.

Devil's Pool

For the adventurous, this pool lets you swim right on the edge of the waterfall, a heart-pounding yet safe experience during the dry season.

Zambezi National Park

Just upstream from the falls, this park offers game drives where you can spot elephants, buffaloes, lions, and more in their natural habitat.

Victoria Falls Bridge

Not only does this iconic bridge connect Zimbabwe and Zambia, but it's also a hub for bungee jumping and stunning views.
