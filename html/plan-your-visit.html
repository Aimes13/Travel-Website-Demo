How To Get There

By Air

Victoria Falls is serviced by the Victoria Falls Airport (VFA), located approximately 20 km from the Falls. Regular flights connect to major cities in Southern Africa, including Johannesburg, Harare, and Livingstone. Shuttle services and taxis are available for transfer to hotels.

By Road

You can drive to Victoria Falls from neighboring countries such as Zambia and Botswana. The Kazungula Border Post is the most common entry point from Botswana. Ensure to have your necessary travel documents ready, including visas, if required.

Travel Tips

When booking flights, consider seasonal fluctuations in prices. Early bookings can often yield the best rates. For detailed flight options, check travel sites like Skyscanner or Kayak.

Getting Around

Local Transport

Once in Victoria Falls, transportation options include taxis, shuttle services, and rental cars. Local taxis ("combis") are available and are a cost-effective way to navigate the area.

Walking

The town is pedestrian-friendly, and many attractions, including the Victoria Falls Rainforest, are within walking distance. Walking is a great way to experience local shops and eateries.

Guided Tours

For a deeper understanding of the region, consider booking guided tours. Many operators offer full-day excursions that include wildlife safaris, sunset cruises, and cultural experiences.

Best Time To Visit

Seasonal Overview

The best time to visit Victoria Falls is during the dry season, from May to October. During this period, the weather is mild, and wildlife is easier to spot. The Falls are spectacular year-round, but water levels vary.

High Water Season

From February to April, the Falls experience high water levels due to seasonal rains, providing breathtaking views, but may reduce visibility due to mist.

Events and Activities

Consider timing your visit to coincide with local festivals or events, such as the Victoria Falls Carnival in December, which offers a unique blend of culture and entertainment.

Visitor Tips

Always check the weather forecast ahead of your visit. The Falls can be misty during the rainy season, impacting visibility.
