The Elephant Camp Hotel: An Exclusive Retreat

The Elephant Camp offers a unique blend of luxury and nature. Guests stay in elegant tented suites, each featuring modern amenities like Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and private plunge pools, while enjoying breathtaking views of the Batoka Gorge and Victoria Falls.
